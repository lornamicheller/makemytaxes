Tax Levi

Levies can really do a lot of damage & even ruin your life. A levy is the IRS's way of getting your immediate attention. What they are saying is, we have tried to communicate with you but you have ignored us. Levies are used to seize your wages & whatever other assets you have. If you own it, they can take it. That includes checking accounts, autos, stocks, bonds, boats, paychecks, & even Social Security checks!

Tax Debt Relief

The IRS views not paying payroll taxes as the cardinal sin of tax delinquency because a large part of the payroll taxes are your employees' withholdings. Not paying your company's payroll taxes is tantamount to stealing your employees' money in the eyes of the IRS.

As a result, penalties for failing to pay your payroll taxes & filing your payroll tax returns on right time are much more severe than other types of penalties. They can drastically multiply the amount you owe in a very short time.

Innocent Spouse Relief

Did you know that you can get out of the tax debt due to the misdeeds or fraud committed by your spouse? Innocent Spouse Relief was designed to alleviate unjust situations where one spouse was clearly the victim of fraud perpetrated by their spouse or ex-spouse.
